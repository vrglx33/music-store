# Music Store Platform - Raw Idea

## Project Description

Create a digital marketplace platform that helps independent artists monetize their music and music lovers discover new content by providing a seamless end-to-end platform for uploading, browsing, and purchasing digital music directly from artists.

## Project Phases & Features

The project has 16 features across 3 phases:

### MVP Phase

1. User Authentication & Authorization
2. Artist Profile & Dashboard
3. Music Upload System
4. Music Catalog Browsing
5. Album & Song Detail Pages
6. Shopping Cart
7. Payment Integration
8. Digital Download Delivery

### Phase 2: Enhanced Discovery & User Experience

9. Search & Filtering
10. Audio Preview Player
11. Purchase History & Re-Downloads
12. Artist Sales Analytics

### Phase 3: Advanced Features & Optimization

13. Advanced Pricing Controls
14. Multi-Format Audio Support
15. Genre-Based Discovery
16. Admin Content Moderation

## Tech Stack

- TypeScript throughout
- React SSR for frontend rendering
- Focus on local development environment with no deployment infrastructure required
